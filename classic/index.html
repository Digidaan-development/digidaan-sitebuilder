About Me

Hi, my name is Daan van Geloven and I am a Software Developer based in the Netherlands. I am a young professional and graduated with a degree in software development in 2021.

In addition to my studies, I currently work part-time as an identity and access management developer for Jumbo supermarkets. I am passionate about using technology to solve problems and am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow as a developer.

I'm specialized in creating custom software solutions that are fast, smart, and simple. My goal is to help businesses like yours streamline their workflow, maximize productivity, and minimize complexity with cutting-edge software solutions.

Whether you need a new website, a custom app, or help with hosting and email systems, I've got you covered. Take a look around and see how I can help elevate your business to the next level.
